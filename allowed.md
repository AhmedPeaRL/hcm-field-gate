# Permitted Artifact Classes

This document does not list products.
It defines *types of existence* allowed to surface.

---

## Allowed Categories

- Raw data structures (csv, json, vectors)
- Pattern libraries
- Tool-like artifacts
- Silent utilities
- Assets usable without narrative
- Bundles that do not self-promote
- Outputs that tolerate misuse

---

## Shared Traits

- No explanation of HCM
- No promise of transformation
- No claim of universality
- No dependency on attention
- No demand for continuity

---

Permission is contextual.
This list is not exhaustive.
